The Coin Knight
A pixel art adventure
By Sullydux

Embark on a shiny quest

The Coin Knight is a platformer game where the knight collects coins and fights slimes. At the end fight the boss!

About the Game

The game is in its alpha stage so not every feature is added yet.

Explore a handcrafted pixel art world filled with challenges and hidden secrets. Coins are scattered throughout the world, waiting to be collected. There are scattered flags that act as checkpoints.

This is a computer game and you can use controllers connected to your computer(untested).

Be a Hero But Also Beware

Save the realm from the slimes and their boss. All of the citizens flee from their presence, so expect to be unaided. Reclaim the looted coins. Restore the kingdom!
